Training & experience:

I am a counsellor who uses a range of techniques from different types of therapy to suit your needs and circumstances. I have a diploma in Adlerian Counselling but use a variety of skills from other modalities such as Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) and Human Givens. I work with various ages, from adolescence through to adulthood. My training helps clients with experiential learning, self-awareness and personal growth. I believe that a counselling relationship should be based on equality, inclusiveness, respect and encouragement. I am a registered member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (MBACP), adhering to their ethical frameworks and committed to ongoing professional development.
